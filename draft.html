Prizes

Components Draft

As part of your project, there are lots of "toys" to play with. However, like life (and sports), we have to work within a budget. As such, each team has $200 virtual dollars to spend from equipment below. Plan wisely and be ready if someone picks your cool toy before someone else. ;)

Draft Process

  1. Teams get 30min to brainstorm.
  2. Organizers randomly pick a team.
  3. That team picks up to $20 worth of items.
    • Alternately, pick 1 expensive item and skips some future turns.
  4. That team is removed from random-pick list.
  5. Repeat 2-4 until all teams have drafted some equipment.
  6. Repeat draft rounds until all gadgets are assigned.
