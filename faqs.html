Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you turn your crazy ideas into real projects. We provide hardware, mentors, fun activities, speakers, workshops, food, friends, and so much more. You take care of hacking; we’ll take care of you.

How do I get there?

The event is partnered with Baker Hughes, however it is at the OSU Endeavor Building.
Address:
215 N Hester St, Stillwater, OK 74078

Do I need a team?

Nope. Teams will be pseudo-randomly assigned. But... you will find out your team a couple days before the event so you can get to know each other.

What can I build?

It will depend on the challeng, but other than than anything your heart desires! We will try to have access to all the hardware technologies you will need. :)

What if I have never coded?

Not a problem. We will do our best to make sure each team has a diverse set of skills, including coding.

Will you provide hardware?

Yes! There’ll be plenty of fun hardware for you to experiment with! We’ll try to put out a list closer to the event so you can see what’s available. Also, feel free to bring your own from home!

I won't be on campus. Can I still participate?

Unfortunately, no. This will be a hands-on challenge. There may be a virtual component to a solution, but a physical prescence is required.

Who can attend?

You are eligible to attend if you're an OSU Undergraduate or Graduata Student.

Have some other questions?

You can email us any additional questions. We are happy to help! Check out the Team page for the appropriate person.
