About

The OSU/BH Hackathon is a 24-hour energy-forward / sustainability/ green hackathon organised by Oklahoma State University and Baker Hughes. The hackathon will see students come together to build an innovative hack to help with energy transition.

Hackers will be a combination of students across any majors, but we expect most with majors related to computer science, environmental engineering, electrical engineering, and mechanical engineering.

The hacks can be pure software, electrical, mechanical... or maybe even a combindation!
